About Onyx Core

AI Infrastructure Built for the Real World

Founded in Cairo, Onyx Core System is an AI infrastructure company. We develop autonomous AI systems that turn data into decisions — across commerce, trade, logistics, and beyond. Our infrastructure is built to adapt to any industry where intelligence creates operational advantage.

Our platform is built on Google Cloud's Vertex AI, enabling machine learning models that continuously learn from global market data, price movements, and trade patterns to give our operations a decisive analytical edge.
